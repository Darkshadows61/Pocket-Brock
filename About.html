Brock (たけし)

Brock, the first leader trainers encounter in the Kanto region is known for a few things; he's the Gym Leader for the Pewter City Gym and sepcializes in Rock-type Pokemon, he wants to become the worlds best Pokemon Breeder, he loves pretty women, he travels with Ash and Misty throughout the Indigio League arc, but most importantly he is a wealth of pokemon knowledge.

Brock is here in your pocket to help you along your pokemon journey by answering some of the imortant questions many trainers have. Here, Brock can help you:

Determine what a type is strong against, weak to, or if it is immune.
Check to see how effective or ineffective an Attack is against one or two Defense Types
